About Agrisoil
Agrisoil is a Canadian international commodity trading company dedicated to connecting global producers with buyers through reliable sourcing, transparent business practices, and efficient supply chain management.
Headquartered in Montreal, Canada, Agrisoil specializes in the international trade of fertilizers and agricultural commodities, serving customers across North America and global markets. By building long-term relationships with trusted producers throughout the Middle East, Central Asia, North Africa, Europe, and the Americas, the company provides dependable access to high-quality products supported by professional logistics and responsive customer service.
Agrisoil was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to simplify international commodity sourcing while creating long-term value for customers, suppliers, and strategic partners. Rather than acting solely as a trading intermediary, the company serves as a strategic supply partner, coordinating procurement, quality assurance, documentation, logistics, and delivery to ensure every transaction is executed efficiently and professionally.
Today, Agrisoil supports customers ranging from fertilizer distributors and agricultural cooperatives to commodity traders, food processors, industrial buyers, and commercial importers. Every transaction is managed with a strong commitment to transparency, reliability, and operational excellence.
Our objective is not simply to sell products but to build trusted partnerships that contribute to stronger agricultural supply chains and long-term business success.

Commercial Terms
Agrisoil supports internationally recognized Incoterms® to provide flexibility across different markets.
FOB (Free on Board)
Customers assume responsibility once cargo is loaded onto the vessel at the designated export port.
Suitable for buyers managing their own freight arrangements.
________________


CFR (Cost and Freight)
Agrisoil arranges ocean transportation to the destination port while the buyer manages cargo insurance.
________________


CIF (Cost, Insurance & Freight)
Agrisoil coordinates freight and marine insurance through the destination port, simplifying procurement for international buyers.
Supporting multiple commercial terms enables Agrisoil to accommodate customers with different logistics capabilities and purchasing preferences.
